# Test Questions for Manager AI Assistant

## English Management Scenarios

### Performance Management
1. How do I give negative feedback to an underperforming employee?
2. One of my team members consistently misses deadlines. What should I do?
3. How do I set clear performance expectations for a new hire?
4. An employee's work quality has declined recently. How should I address this?
5. How do I handle an employee who argues with feedback?

### Team Dynamics & Conflict
6. Two team members are in constant conflict. What's my role as their manager?
7. How do I deal with a team member who undermines others?
8. My team lacks motivation after a failed project. How do I rebuild morale?
9. How do I handle gossip and negativity spreading in my team?
10. One employee dominates meetings while others stay silent. What should I do?

### Leadership & Delegation
11. How do I decide which tasks to delegate and to whom?
12. A team member keeps asking for my approval on everything. How do I build their confidence?
13. How do I balance being supportive with holding people accountable?
14. What's the best way to give recognition and praise?
15. How do I prepare my team for change in the organization?

## Edge Cases & Boundaries

### Legal/HR Redirect Cases
26. Can I fire someone for being late to work repeatedly?
27. What are the legal requirements for terminating an employee?
28. An employee filed a harassment complaint against another team member. How do I handle this?
29. Can I ask personal questions about an employee's health issues affecting work?
30. What documentation do I need before firing someone for poor performance?

### Out of Scope
31. How do I optimize my team's productivity using blockchain technology?
32. Tell me about quantum physics applications in management.
33. What's the best programming language for my development team?
34. How do I calculate compound interest for employee bonuses?
35. Can you help me write code for our performance tracking system?

38. Which framework should I use for handling remote team conflicts?
39. Do you have any frameworks for managing during organizational restructuring?

## Quality Checkpoints

For each response, verify:
- ✅ Responds in same language as question
- ✅ Uses proper response structure (Quick Answer, Framework, Action Steps, Considerations)
- ✅ Cites specific framework with exact [Framework - Section] format
- ✅ Provides actionable steps, not generic advice
- ✅ Handles edge cases appropriately (legal → HR, out of scope → redirect)
- ✅ No hallucinated frameworks
- ✅ Professional, empathetic tone
- ✅ Appropriate length (2-4 paragraphs)

## Success Criteria
- 90%+ questions answered appropriately
- 95%+ framework citations accurate
- 100% edge cases handled correctly
- 100% language matching
- Response time feels natural (not too long/short)
