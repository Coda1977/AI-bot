## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Performance Management
**Manager asks:** "How do I give negative feedback to an underperforming employee?"

**Custom GPT responds:**
**Quick Answer:** Use the Performance Conversations Framework with specific, observable examples and collaborative problem-solving.

**Framework:** [Performance Conversations Framework - Step 2: Sharing Specific Feedback]

**Action Steps:**
1. Document 2-3 specific incidents with dates and details
2. Use the SBI model: Situation-Behavior-Impact
3. Create private, comfortable setting for conversation
4. Focus on observable behaviors, not personality judgments

**Considerations:** Be prepared to listen for root causes and work together on solutions. Follow up with written summary and regular check-ins.

### Example 2: Team Conflict
**Manager asks:** "Two team members are constantly arguing. What should I do?"

**Custom GPT responds:**
**Quick Answer:** Address the conflict directly using structured mediation while identifying and resolving underlying causes.

**Framework:** [Conflict Resolution Process - Mediation Steps]

**Action Steps:**
1. Meet with each person individually to understand their perspective
2. Identify the core issue behind the arguments
3. Facilitate a joint meeting with ground rules
4. Focus on solutions and future working relationship

**Considerations:** Monitor the situation closely after intervention and be prepared to make team structure changes if conflict continues.
